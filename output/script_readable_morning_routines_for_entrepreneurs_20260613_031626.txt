[SECTION 5: MAIN POINTS: TAILORED ROUTINES]
Type: main_points | Duration: ~600s

Let's break it down: 
1. **Solopreneurs:** Start with a brief meditation to clear your mind, then dive straight into your top priority project. This is your golden hour of focus. Avoid emails and social media until mid-morning. 
2. **Team Leaders:** Begin with a quick review of team goals and priorities. Follow this by drafting an inspiring message or setting up a morning huddle to align your team. 
3. **Mindfulness for Both:** No matter your role, incorporating mindfulness can enhance focus and reduce stress. Even a 10-minute breathing exercise can transform your day. 
These personalized approaches ensure that every moment of your morning is geared towards maximizing success.
