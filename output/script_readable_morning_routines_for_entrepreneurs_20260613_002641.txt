[SECTION 5: CRAFTING YOUR 5-MINUTE MORNING ROUTINE]
Type: main_points | Duration: ~300s
Visual: Each tip transitions with a slide showing the time and activity. Example: 'Minute 1: Breathing Exercise' with a timer animation.

First, let's start with a quick mental clarity exercise. Spend one minute focusing on your breath. This simple practice helps center your mind and eliminate morning fog. Next, move on to a one-minute stretch. Just a few dynamic stretches can help wake up your body and increase blood flow. For the third minute, hydrate. A glass of water kickstarts your metabolism and hydrates your body after a night's rest. Fourth, simplify your breakfast. A quick, nutritious option like a banana or a handful of nuts can provide immediate energy. Finally, spend your last minute looking at your top goal for the day. Visualize completing it successfully. This mental exercise aligns your mind with your priorities.
