[SECTION 5: THE STEP-BY-STEP 5-MINUTE ROUTINE]

First up, hydration—start with a glass of water. This simple act jumpstarts your metabolism and wakes up your body. 

Next, mindfulness. Spend one minute focusing on your breath or setting a clear intention for the day. This isn’t about meditation; it’s about clarity and focus. 

Then, a quick stretch. Two minutes of stretching can relieve tension and boost your energy levels significantly. 

Finally, organize your priorities. Take one minute to jot down your top tasks for the day. This isn’t a to-do list; it’s a focus list. 

In just five minutes, you’re not only preparing your body and mind for the day ahead but also reclaiming your time and energy.

These small actions compound, setting a foundation for a successful day.
